Policies

Payment We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, Paypal, Money Orders, and Personal Checks. You can choose your payment type on the last page of the checkout.

Personal checks have a 10 day hold before your order is shipped.



Shipping All in stock orders are shipped within 1 business week of being ordered. When item has shipped your credit card will be charged accrodingly.

Shipping within the United States is free for ground delivery.

International shipping varies by country. The best thing to do is to go through the checkout process. Before you are asked for any payment information it will tell you the shipping charges for your address.


Returns If something doesn't fit right or isn't what you expected just let us know and we will gladly take the product back for a full refund. You must contact us within 7 days of receiving product and we will issue you a return authorization (RA) number.

All products that are being returned must be have approval first.

Product that is returned with out prior & proper approval will be refused.

Our customer service hours are 10am-6pm eastern time. toll free 1.800.627.4980

All approved returns will be logged and RA # will be given.

Shpping costs will not be returned. Customer pays to have product shipped back to us.


Exchanges There is no restocking fee for exchanges. Once the product has returned to Distro.Y we then send out another product. We do not send out product before the original product has been returned. If product is returned in less condition then disclosed we reserve all rights to refuse the exchange.

Any and all shipping charges are paid for by the customer.


Stock Items in stock usually ship same day or next day. We try to keep everything in stock, but with 30 designs x 5 sizes each... well you do the math; ts tough to have everything on hand. We print everything in house, so even out of stock items usually ship within a week. If there is a delay longer than that we will contact you.


Privacy We are committed to protecting your privacy at DISTRO.Y. We will not collect any personal information from you that you do not volunteer, and we are the sole owner of all information collected on this site. We do not sell, share, or rent this information to others in any way that we have not mentioned in this statement.


Cookies Like many other Web sites, DISTRO.Y uses cookies to identify members in order to keep items in your shopping cart and keep you logged in. You will not be able to purchase items from the site without cookies enabled.


Questions Any questions or comments you have can be sent to us at support@distroy.com.
